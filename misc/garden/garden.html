Distinctive, rich, fruity tomato flavor. Relatively smooth, 3- to 5 ounce tomatoes. Indeterminate. Heirloom. Organically grown. Brandywine: Luscious, heirloom flavor as "very rich, loud, and distinctively spicy." Produces tomatoes that can weigh about one pound. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Yellow Brandywine: An orange version of Brandywine. This potato-leaved variety can be finicky to grow, with roughly-shaped fruit one year and smooth the next. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Heirloom. Cherokee Purple: Medium-large, flattened globe, 8 to 12 ounce. fruits. Color is dusky pink with dark shoulders. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Heirloom. Cherokee Green: Medium-sized, 8 ounce. green fruits. Acquire some yellowish-orange color on the blossom end when ripe. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Heirloom. Rose: Deep pink and smoother than Brandywine, Every bit as meaty and flavorful. Fruits weigh 10 ounces. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Great White: Big yellow-white fruits with mild flavor. The 12 ounce fruit is meaty with few seeds, a mild non-acid flavor and creamy texture. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Heirloom. Moskvich: Fruits are deep red and cold tolerant with a rich tomato flavor. 4 - 6 ounce fruits with a small stem scar. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Heirloom. Valencia: Sunny orange fruits with full tomato flavor. Round, smooth fruits average 8 - 10 ounces. This mid-season tomato among the best for flavor and texture. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Heirloom. Striped German: The flat, medium to large, variably ribbed-shoulder tomatoes are shaded yellow and red. 12 ounce fruits. Indeterminate. Organically grown. Heirloom.
